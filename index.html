Discovery Center Mission

Featured Mission

Implement Event-Driven Agentic AI with SAP Joule and SAP Advanced Event Mesh

Duration: 2–4 weeks Experience Level: Intermediate Updated: 2025

The Challenge

Business events are processed in batch. Orders wait hours. Maintenance is reactive. AI agents lack real-time context and governed connectivity.

The Outcome

Joule agents subscribe to live S/4HANA events, reason over real-time data from SAP IBP, TM, Ariba, and SuccessFactors, then publish governed transactions back through Advanced Event Mesh.
